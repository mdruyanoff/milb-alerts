Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

When you opt in to MiLB Game Alerts, we collect:

  • Mobile phone number: Provided when you reply YES to the opt-in message.
  • Consent status: Whether you have opted in, opted out, or have a pending request, and the timestamps of these actions.
  • Player preferences: Which baseball players you have chosen to follow.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and player preferences are used solely to deliver the baseball game alert messages you have subscribed to. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the alerts you requested.

No Sharing of Mobile Information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties. This includes your phone number, opt-in status, and any information collected through the text messaging opt-in process.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use Twilio as our messaging platform to deliver text messages. Twilio processes your phone number solely for the purpose of message delivery on our behalf. Twilio's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy: twilio.com/legal/privacy.

We retrieve baseball statistics from MLB's Stats API. No personal information is transmitted to or shared with MLB.

No other third parties receive your personal information.

Data Storage and Security

Your phone number and preferences are stored in private configuration files on a secured server. Data is not stored in any third-party database, cloud analytics service, or advertising platform. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including restricting server access. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

Your information is retained only for as long as you are subscribed to the Service. Upon opting out (replying STOP), your consent status is updated and no further messages are sent. You may request complete deletion of all your data by contacting the service operator.

Your Rights

  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
  • Request deletion of all your data by contacting the service operator.
  • Request access to the data we hold about you.

Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your data rights, reply HELP to any message from this Service or contact the service operator directly at the phone number from which messages are sent.
