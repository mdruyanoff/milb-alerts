How It Works

MiLB Game Alerts monitors live baseball games across all Minor League levels (AAA, AA, High-A, Single-A) and MLB Spring Training. When a game finishes, subscribers receive a text message with their tracked players' stats within 60 seconds.

Example alert (hitter):

Esteury Ruiz MLB/MIA @ HOU 1-3 | R, BB, SB Reply STOP to unsubscribe

Example alert (pitcher):

Germán Márquez MLB/SD vs CHC (W) 3.0 IP, 2 H, 0 R, 0 ER, 3 K, 0 BB, 0 HR Reply STOP to unsubscribe

Message frequency varies based on game schedules and the number of players you follow. During the baseball season (March–September), you may receive multiple messages per day on days when your tracked players have games.

How to Subscribe (Opt-In)

MiLB Game Alerts is an invite-only service. To subscribe:

  1. You are added as a recipient by the service operator, who registers your phone number and the players you wish to follow.
  2. You receive a one-time opt-in text message from the MiLB Game Alerts number:
    MiLB Game Alerts: You've been invited to receive real-time minor league baseball player stats via text. Reply YES to subscribe or NO to decline. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP anytime to cancel.
  3. Reply YES to subscribe and begin receiving alerts.
  4. Reply NO to decline. You will not receive any messages.

You will not receive any alert messages until you reply YES. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. You may opt out at any time.

How to Unsubscribe (Opt-Out)

Every message includes the line: Reply STOP to unsubscribe

Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe at any time by replying YES.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for support information. You may also contact the service operator directly at the number messages are sent from.

Costs

The MiLB Game Alerts service is free. Standard message and data rates from your carrier may apply.

Privacy

Your phone number is used solely to deliver the baseball alerts you subscribed to. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our full Privacy Policy.
