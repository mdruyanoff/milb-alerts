# Terms of Service — MiLB Game Alerts

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Service Description

MiLB Game Alerts ("Service") sends text message notifications containing Minor League and Major League Baseball player statistics immediately after games conclude. The Service is provided for informational and entertainment purposes only.

## 2. Consent & Opt-In

By replying **YES** to our opt-in message, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages at the phone number you provided. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

## 3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on game schedules and the number of players you follow. During the baseball season (approximately March–September), you may receive multiple messages per day.

## 4. Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details. The Service itself is free.

## 5. Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe by replying **YES**.

## 6. Help

Reply **HELP** to any message for assistance, or contact the Service operator directly.
