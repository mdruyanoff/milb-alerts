# Privacy Policy — MiLB Game Alerts

*Last updated: March 2026*

## Information We Collect

- **Phone number**: Provided when you opt in to receive text messages.
- **Consent status**: Whether you have opted in, opted out, or have a pending request.
- **Player preferences**: Which players you have chosen to follow.

## How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send you baseball game alerts that you have requested. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the alerts you subscribed to.

## Data Storage

Your phone number and preferences are stored in a private configuration file on a secured server. Data is not stored in any third-party database or cloud analytics service.

## Third-Party Services

We use **Twilio** to deliver text messages. Twilio processes your phone number to deliver messages on our behalf. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their handling of your data: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

We retrieve baseball statistics from **MLB's Stats API**. No personal information is shared with MLB.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or personal information with any third party, except as required to deliver messages through Twilio.

## Data Retention

Your information is retained for as long as you are subscribed. Upon opting out (replying STOP), your consent status is updated and no further messages are sent. You may request complete deletion of your data by contacting the Service operator.

## Your Rights

- **Opt out** at any time by replying STOP.
- **Request deletion** of your data by contacting the operator.
- **Request access** to the data we hold about you.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including restricting access to the server where data is stored. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reply HELP to any message or contact the Service operator directly.
